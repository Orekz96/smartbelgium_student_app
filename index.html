About Us

SmartBelgium is your go-to platform for all things related to studying in Belgium. From city guides to tips on student life, we have you covered.

SmartBelgium is your trusted guide to navigating life as an international student in Belgium — designed by students, for students. We know that arriving in a new country can be overwhelming, especially when you're juggling academics, housing, legal paperwork, and trying to find your place in a new culture. That’s why we created a warm, easy-to-use web platform that answers the big questions students have when they first land in cities like Leuven, Brussels, and Ghent. From getting a Belgian SIM card to finding affordable housing, from setting up a student bank account to joining exciting student events, SmartBelgium offers step-by-step tips, city-specific advice, and helpful resources all in one place. We focus on the things that matter most: Settling in fast and stress-free Avoiding costly mistakes Feeling connected and informed
